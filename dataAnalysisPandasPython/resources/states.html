List of U.S. states

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

A U.S. state is one of the states of the United States of America. Four states (Kentucky, Massachusetts, Pennsylvania, and Virginia) are officially named a "Commonwealth" rather than a "State." The earliest state became one on January the second, 1788, and the latest, Hawaii on August the twenty-first, 1959.

The states are labeled with their U.S. postal abbreviations, their founding date and capitals.

In addition to the states, several other areas belong to the United States:

  • AS American Samoa, Territory
  • DC District of Columbia, which is the seat of the Federal government
  • GU Guam, Territory
  • MP Northern Mariana Islands, Commonwealth
  • PR Puerto Rico, Territories
  • VI the United States Virgin Islands, Territory
